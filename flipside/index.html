Flipside

You can play the game here.

Flipside is a small platformer made in Unity, developed in 48 hours for the 2016 Global Game Jam. The game is made of of different sections (biomes if you will), each with their own unique style, music and orientation of gravity.

You can view our page on GGJ here.

  • Ben Brinkman
